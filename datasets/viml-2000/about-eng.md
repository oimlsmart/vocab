# VIML (2000) — International Vocabulary of Legal Metrology

The **International Vocabulary of Legal Metrology (VIML)** is published by the
International Organization of Legal Metrology (OIML) as **OIML V 1:2000**.

This is the **2000 edition**, superseded by [OIML V 1:2013](/vocab/viml-2013/concept/0.01).
It provides 44 definitions of terms used in legal metrology across 4 sections.

## Structure

| Section | Title |
|---------|-------|
| 1 | Basic terms in legal metrology |
| 2 | Legal metrology activities |
| 3 | Documents and marks within legal metrology |
| 4 | Units and measuring instruments |

## About OIML

The [International Organization of Legal Metrology (OIML)](https://www.oiml.org)
is an intergovernmental treaty organization that promotes the global harmonization
of legal metrology procedures.
