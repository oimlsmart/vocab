# VIM (2010) — International Vocabulary of Metrology

The **International Vocabulary of Metrology (VIM)** is co-published by the
Joint Committee for Guides in Metrology (JCGM) as **JCGM 200:2010** and by
OIML as **OIML V 2-200:2010**.

This is the **2010 edition**, superseded by [OIML V 2-200:2012](/oiml-vocab/vim/concept/1.1).
It provides 143 concept definitions across 5 chapters with corrected definitions
from the 2007 edition.

## Structure

| Chapter | Title |
|---------|-------|
| 1 | Quantities and units |
| 2 | Measurements |
| 3 | Devices for measurement |
| 4 | Properties of measuring devices |
| 5 | Measurement standards (Etalons) |

## About JCGM

The [Joint Committee for Guides in Metrology (JCGM)](https://www.bipm.org/en/committees/jc/jcgm)
maintains the VIM and the Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement (GUM).
The JCGM brings together BIPM, IEC, IFCC, ILAC, ISO, IUPAC, IUPAP, and OIML.
