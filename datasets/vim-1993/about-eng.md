# VIM (1993) — International Vocabulary of Metrology

The **International Vocabulary of Metrology (VIM)** was originally published as
**ISO Guide 99:1993** (also known as **ISO/TAG 4:1993**), co-published by OIML as
**OIML V 2:1993** and by the JCGM predecessor as **JCGM 200:1993**.

This is the **1993 edition**, superseded by [OIML V 2-200:2007](/vocab/vim-2007/concept/1.1).
It provides 120 concept definitions across 6 chapters. This edition uses a different
chapter structure from later editions, with separate chapters for "Measurement results"
and "Measuring instruments" instead of the consolidated "Devices for measurement" chapter.

**Note:** This dataset was extracted from OCR-scanned documents and may contain
typographical errors. Manual review is recommended.

## About JCGM

The [Joint Committee for Guides in Metrology (JCGM)](https://www.bipm.org/en/committees/jc/jcgm)
maintains the VIM and the Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement (GUM).
The JCGM brings together BIPM, IEC, IFCC, ILAC, ISO, IUPAC, IUPAP, and OIML.
